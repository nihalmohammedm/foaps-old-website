FAQ

Question & answer

How long will it take to install and start using Foaps?

The duration of installation will depend on the platform you decide to integrate with Foaps. Usually, it only takes 3-5 working days to complete the integration process and to receive orders on our system.

Does Foaps charge commission on orders?

No, Foaps works on a subscription basis and we don’t charge a commission on orders.

What is the implementation process for Foaps?

The implementation process is quite simple.

  • Firstly, create an account on Foaps.
  • Choose a single or multiple online food delivery platforms that you wish to integrate with Foaps.
  • Then, Foaps will share your restaurant details with selected delivery partners to begin the integration process.
  • Once the process is completed by the delivery partner, you will be sent a test order to make sure if your restaurant is ready to go live.
  • Once the confirmation is received from your end and the order status is successfully updated at the restaurant, the delivery partners will be informed of the same online food delivery orders on Foaps.
Are there any extra charges for help and support?

No, help and support are completely free. We truly believe that customer support is our business’s heart and soul, and a dedicated support team is always accessible for providing prompt support.

How do I make payments?

Foaps will automatically take funds from the customer account on the specific date chosen by the customer every month.

How secure are my payments?

Foaps is using a secure channel like Razorpay as the payment gateway. For more details you may please check their terms & conditions to know more about their services.

Can I change my plan at a later time?

Yes, you can change your plan anytime from our account setting page.
